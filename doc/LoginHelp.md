# Sign in

[简体中文](LoginHelp_ZH.md)

1. Open the AI Algo Helper tool window.
2. Click **Sign in**.
3. Complete the normal LeetCode sign-in flow in the built-in browser dialog.

The plugin does not collect or store your LeetCode username or password. After LeetCode confirms the sign-in, the plugin stores the resulting session cookies through JetBrains PasswordSafe and closes the dialog.

If the page cannot be loaded, check the IDE proxy settings and network access to the selected LeetCode site, then reopen the dialog.
